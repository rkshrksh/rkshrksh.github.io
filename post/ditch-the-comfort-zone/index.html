Ditch the Comfort Zone

28 May 2019

You have to get out of your comfort zone to achieve something.

You must have heard or read this line many times in books or some random youtube videos.

The sense of this line is very simple and true. We have to leave our comfort zone, to move ahead in our life. But how many of us really do it. Some of us try and succeed. Some try for a bit but come back to their comfort zone as soon as they face hard challenge or something that needs some extra work.

And then there are those who don’t even try. I don’t have any magic medicine for these people. The only person who can help them is they themselves. So if you want to achieve your goals or dreams, than start with getting out of your comfort zone. Comfort zone is the place that fears change. But change is important. Without change we can not improve, and without improvement, how can we achieve something that needs hard work.

Sometimes we lose the drive to achieve something in between the struggle. That’s normal and happens with almost all of us. You can’t lose your hope at this point. You have to continue your hustle and not stop until you have achieved your dreams.

You are the only person in this whole world that can help you to achieve your goals and Also the only person who can stop you from doing the same.

So fight yourself and get out of the Comfort Zone. Start your Hustle. Make yourself listen to your good self. Than only you will be able to reach your goals.

Get out and do something. Go to the gym. Start working on that project. Read that book. Write something. Do anything that gets you closer to your goal, every single day.

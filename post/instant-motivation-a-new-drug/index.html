Instant Motivation a New Drug

05 Apr 2019

These days wherever you look, you can find articles, blogs, videos, vlogs about motivation. That will try to motivate you to achieve your goals. When you read a blog or watch a motivational video, you feel better about yourself and start thinking that you can do it. You can achieve it.

But what after that. What happens the next hour or the next day. Do you really start working on your dreams to achieve it OR you just come back looking for that sweet candy of motivation. Yeah right, you are reading articles or watching a video to feel good about yourself again while lying on your comfortable couch or bed.

That’s what is wrong with most of us or with this new Instant motivation industry. When you consume this content you feel motivated to achieve your goals. But rather than start working to achieve it, you just want to think that you can do it. Yes, you can do it, I also say that but only after you start working for it. Not by just consuming this so called motivational shit, That is supposed to make you feel better about yourself only.

According to me this Instant Motivation is like a drug, a new age drug, which we all are addicted to. I also found myself, watching some videos on YouTube, one after another. But that’s not productive, we need to move past that and do actual shit to achieve our goals.

Watch motivational videos, read those blogs but not one after another. When you feel motivated just start working towards your goals. Don’t come back the next day in need of some more dose of the drugs.

Keep working day after day to really achieve it, that is the only way we can achieve our goals and move ahead in our life. Break the addiction of this Instant Motivation drug. You don’t need a daily dose of motivation if you really want to achieve your goals. You just need to start working towards the goals and motivation will come by itself.

What You Believe is what you achieve.

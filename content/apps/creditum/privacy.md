# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Data Collection
Creditum does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information you enter into the app remains on your device.

## Local Storage
- All transactions, person names, and notes are stored locally in a SQLite database on your device
- Your data is never uploaded automatically
- You can export your data manually using the Backup feature

## Permissions

### Contact Access (Optional)
- Creditum may request access to your contacts to help you add people quickly
- This permission is optional and can be revoked at any time through your device settings
- Contact data is used only for autofilling names and is never stored or transmitted

### Storage Access (For Backups)
- Required only when you choose to export or import backup files
- You select the exact location where backups are saved

## Third-Party Services
Creditum does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy
If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in the app's about page.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, please contact me.

## Features
- **Dashboard** - View all people and your total balance at a glance
- **Transactions** - Keep track of every IOU with detailed history
- **Backup & Restore** - Export and import your data to any location
- **Theming** - Choose from Light, Dark, or System themes
 
## Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Your financial information never leaves your device unless you explicitly create a backup.

## Privacy
Creditum respects your privacy:
- No account required
- No data collection or analytics
- No internet connection required
- Contact access is optional (for importing contacts)
